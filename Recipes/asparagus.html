Roasted Asparagus with Parmesan

Fresh asparagus spears are simply seasoned with garlic, topped with shredded Parmesan cheese, and baked until tender and sweet in this easy spring side dish.

Parmesan Asparagus Ingredients:

Oil

This recipe calls for olive oil cooking spray, but you can easily grease your casserole dish with regular ol' olive oil.

Asparagus

Buy plump, firm asparagus stalks with tightly closed tips. The color should be vibrant and unfaded, while the stalks should be roughly the same size for even cooking.

Parmesan

Freshly grated Parmesan cheese is best, but pre-shredded will work in a pinch.

Sea Salt

Opt for a coarse sea salt for the best results. Kosher salt will work if that's all you have on hand.

Garlic Powder

Just a pinch of garlic powder adds bold, herbaceous flavor to this Parmesan-topped roasted asparagus.
